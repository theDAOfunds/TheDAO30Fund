  • COIN
    FORMULAS

    The "TARGET" percentage of any token is calculated by taking the token's MarketCap (in Billions of U$D) and deviding it by the current price (in U$D) of one EOS Token. This yeilds a Market Capitalization expressed in terms of EOS Tokens. The sqare root of this "MarketCap in EOS Tokens" is the amount of EOS token value that the DAO30 FUND should hold of that token for every one DAO Token sold to an investor, hence its the "TARGET".

    As the MarketCaps of tokens change, these amounts will vary, hence the TARGET percentage is chased after with new purchases.

    The "ACTUAL" amounts are used to both determine which Tokens to sell and what the "sale price" is when a DAO30 Token is sold. Just like when purchasing, when selling, the DAO Fund will sell the Tokens it has in excess first.

    In this manner the buying and selling of tokens is minimized and the ACTUAL percentages will keep aproaching the TARGET levels programed for by the DAO30 Governace Dapp.

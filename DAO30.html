  • COIN
    FORMULAS


    The "OPTIMAL" percentage of any token is calculated by taking the token's MarketCap (in Billions of U$D) and deviding it by the current price (in U$D) of one EOS Token. This yeilds a Market Capitalization expressed in terms of EOS Tokens. The sqare root of this "MarketCap in EOS Tokens" is the amount of EOS token value that the DAO30 FUND should hold in that token for every one DAO Token sold to an investor. As the MarketCaps of tokens change, these amounts will vary, hence the "OPTIMAL" percen- tage is chased after with new purchases and the "ACTUAL" amounts are use when cashing out a DAO30 Token. In this manner the buying and selling of tokens is minimized and the ACTUAL percentages will keep aproaching the OPTIMAL levels programed for by the DAO30 Governace Dapp.
